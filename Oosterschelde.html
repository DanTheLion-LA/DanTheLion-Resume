Oosterschelde VR - Underwater Experience

Project Overview

“The Oosterschelde VR project was set up to create an underwater experience that shows the unique ecosystem of the estuary. The goal was to build a 360° interactive video that could run on limited VR hardware while still feeling immersive. This meant working with strict technical limitations and finding ways to keep the visuals optimized without losing the atmosphere of being underwater. The project covered the whole process from early design choices to the final playable build.”
