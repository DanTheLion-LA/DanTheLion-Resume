Oosterschelde VR — Underwater Experience

Intro

“The Oosterschelde VR project is an immersive underwater experience designed to bring the unique ecosystem of the Dutch estuary to life. Working within strict technical and hardware limitations, I guided the project from start to finish — creating optimized 3D environments, implementing interactive elements, and ensuring smooth performance on VR hardware. The goal was to make the beauty and fragility of the Oosterschelde accessible to a broad audience in a way that feels both educational and memorable.”
