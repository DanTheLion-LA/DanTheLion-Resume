Python Autorigger - Web-based auto rigging system

Intro

“During my graduation, I developed the TPose Autorigger (v1.0) — a web-based automatic rigging system designed for remote motion-capture pipelines. I built it to streamline character rigging in real time, enabling artists to upload raw mocap data and get fully rigged models with minimal manual intervention. From prototyping and benchmarking to user testing and iteration, I led the project end-to-end — blending VFX, usability research, and pipeline engineering to deliver a responsive, production-ready tool.”
